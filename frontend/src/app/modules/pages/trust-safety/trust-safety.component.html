Trust and Safety

Reputation matters

Businesses earn great reviews on Dinespotting the same way they build their reputations in the community: by excellent food recipes, services, and customer experiences. This is because () works hard to feature content that presents real experiences that customers feel motivated to share.

Authenticity & Reliability

Under Dinespotting policies, businesses are not allowed to ask their customers for reviews, and customers can only write about their first-hand experiences. Also, we use automated recommendation software designed to identify and recommend the most trusted content.

Providing Right Information

Fake news and ambiguous behavior are unfortunately common on the web. We have teams and policies to secure our community of consumers and businesses and prevent any potential misfortune.

Data you Want to Know.

Numerous factors are crucial when considering a business. At Dinespotting, we offer essential information to help customers feel comfortable with their decision.
