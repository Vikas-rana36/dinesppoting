From Where it all Started!

DineSpotting was founded in December 2021 while one of the co-founders, Rashid, was touring in New York City. As a tourist Rashid encountered a few challenges while trying to locate the top-rated restaurantsaround.

He had to google five restaurants, compare ratings, and read reviews from three different sources. What was intended to be a simple exercise turned out to be a difficult and time-consuming process.

During this troublesome course, the idea of DineSpotting came into being.

Dinespotting was founded by ‘foodies for foodies’ in 2021!

Our Mission

DineSpotting serves as a one-stop website for all our Dinespotters. By streamlining your restaurant search, we aim to ensure that you never have to eat at a subpar restaurant again!

It looks after your restaurant needs, where you can read good food reviews and discover the best-reviewed restaurants near you.

Discover your favorite restaurants

Our sleek layout makes it easy to locate exactly what you're searching for. We classify most-reviewed restaurants based on proximity and cuisine type, so whether you are craving authentic Thai or Italian food, DineSpotting has something for you!
