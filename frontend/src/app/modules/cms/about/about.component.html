From Where it All Started...

Dinespotting was founded in December 2021, when Rashid—one of the co-founders—was a tourist in New York City.

For a tourist like Rashid, finding the best restaurants around him was difficult due to several reasons: he had to google around 5 different restaurants, compare ratings from 3 different sources and read several reviews from various sources.

What's supposed to be a simple process ended up being a difficult and time-consuming process.

It was during this troublesome process that the idea of Dinespotting came into existence. Dinespotting aims to eliminate the troublesome process of finding restaurants by providing all the information needed in one place.
